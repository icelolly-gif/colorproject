Gardenns and gardeners come in all size, shapes, and needs. We may be short or tall, left-handed or right-handed. we may be starting a few planterr boxes on the deck or growng entire rows of vegatables in the backyard. Thus the tool that work for one person may not work for another

Complicating matters even more are tools that can serve multiple purpose. For example, some people might like a hand hoe for clearing weeds while otherrs use it for creating furrows for bulbs. A weeder is made for pulling up taprootsbut its narrow head also makes it ideal fr dibbling holes for seeds. What can be agreed on is that having the right tool for your particular needs is essential for your sanity and syccess in the garden.

Use this shopping list as a guide of what might be useful when you hit the ground running. It's best to "try on" tools in person so you can get a feel for what's comfortable in your hand or proporitionate to your height.
